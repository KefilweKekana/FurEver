Boarding Animal Form – Terms & Indemnity

Important Information

  • All animals must be up to date with vaccinations. Proof of current vaccinations must be presented at the time of booking.
  • No animal will be admitted without proof of vaccination.
  • Animals that are not sterilized may be sterilized at the owner's cost while in the care of the SPCA.
  • Animals must be collected on or before the agreed date. A late collection fee will apply.
  • The SPCA reserves the right to obtain veterinary treatment for any boarded animal in the event of illness or injury. All costs will be for the owner's account.
  • Feeding will be as per SPCA standard diet. Special dietary requirements must be communicated in advance and food supplied by the owner.
  • While every care is taken, the SPCA cannot be held responsible for any illness, injury, or death that may occur during the boarding period.
  • Boarding fees are payable in advance and are non-refundable.
  • Animals not collected within 7 days after the agreed date will be deemed abandoned and become the property of the SPCA.
