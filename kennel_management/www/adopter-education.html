The 3-3-3 Rule of Adoption

📅 First 3 Days

Your pet may feel overwhelmed. They might not eat, drink, or want to explore. Hiding is completely normal. Be patient and give them space.

📅 First 3 Weeks

They're starting to settle in and learn your routines. Their personality is beginning to show. Keep things consistent and calm.

📅 First 3 Months

Now fully comfortable! Their true personality emerges, the bond deepens, and trust is fully established. You're family now! 🎉
